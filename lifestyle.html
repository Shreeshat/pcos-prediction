Lifestyle Management for PCOS

Discover effective lifestyle strategies to manage PCOS symptoms, improve overall health, and enhance quality of life.

Nutrition & Diet

  • Low glycemic index foods
  • Balanced macronutrients
  • Anti-inflammatory foods
  • Regular meal timing

Exercise & Activity

  • 150 mins moderate exercise/week
  • Strength training 2-3x/week
  • HIIT workouts
  • Daily walking routine

Stress Management

  • Meditation & mindfulness
  • Yoga and breathing exercises
  • Adequate sleep (7-9 hours)
  • Relaxation techniques

Sleep & Recovery

  • Consistent sleep schedule
  • Sleep-friendly environment
  • Limit screen time before bed
  • Relaxing bedtime routine

Comprehensive Lifestyle Strategies

Dietary Guidelines

Foods to Include

  • • High-fiber vegetables and fruits
  • • Lean proteins (chicken, fish, tofu)
  • • Healthy fats (avocado, nuts, olive oil)
  • • Complex carbohydrates (oats, quinoa)

Foods to Limit

  • • Refined sugars and carbohydrates
  • • Processed and fast foods
  • • Sugary beverages
  • • Excessive dairy products

Exercise Program

Weekly Schedule

  • Monday: Strength training (30-45 mins)
  • Tuesday: Cardio (30 mins) + Yoga
  • Wednesday: Active rest (walking)
  • Thursday: HIIT workout (20-30 mins)
  • Friday: Strength training (30-45 mins)
  • Weekend: Active recovery

Mental Wellness

  • Practice daily gratitude journaling
  • Connect with support communities
  • Set realistic health goals
  • Celebrate small victories

Long-term Health

  • Regular health check-ups
  • Monitor symptoms and progress
  • Stay informed about PCOS research
  • Build sustainable habits

Comprehensive Treatment Guide

Medical Treatments

Hormonal Therapy

  • Birth Control Pills: Regulate menstrual cycles and reduce androgen levels
  • Progestin Therapy: Induce periods and protect endometrial health
  • Anti-androgen Medications: Reduce hair growth and acne

Insulin-Sensitizing Drugs

  • Metformin: Improves insulin sensitivity and may aid weight loss
  • GLP-1 Receptor Agonists: Help with weight management
  • Inositol Supplements: Natural approach to improve insulin function

Fertility Treatments

Ovulation Induction

  • Clomiphene Citrate: First-line treatment to stimulate ovulation
  • Letrozole: Often more effective than clomiphene for PCOS
  • Gonadotropins: Injectable hormones for resistant cases

Advanced Options

  • In Vitro Fertilization (IVF): For complex fertility issues
  • Laparoscopic Ovarian Drilling: Surgical option to restore ovulation
  • Oocyte Cryopreservation: Fertility preservation for future

Complementary Therapies

  • Herbal Supplements:

    Spearmint tea, cinnamon, berberine for symptom management

  • Acupuncture:

    May help regulate cycles and reduce stress

  • Nutritional Supplements:

    Vitamin D, omega-3s, N-acetylcysteine for metabolic health

Surgical Options

  • Ovarian Drilling:

    Laparoscopic procedure to restore ovulation in resistant cases

  • Bariatric Surgery:

    For severe obesity with PCOS to improve metabolic health

  • Laser Therapies:

    For hirsutism management and cosmetic concerns
